StudyBot: an AI Based ChatBot

Jun 2020 – Jun 2021

Currently if students want to find some information regarding topics from their syllabus, they will have to manually search through reference books which is a tedious task or do a google search which is not able to provide the solution as a single accurate result. So, we propose a chatbot which will provide a simple interface using Python and YAML as Knowledgebase where user can input his query and the system will provide single accurate result for an inputted query as opposed to Google's multi-sourced results.

DR.TOM: an AI Based Pet Diagnostics System

Jun 2018 – Apr 2019

In today’s world, owning to the heavy workload, Pet owners are having huge amount of stress in their lives, That they might not be able to give proper attention to their pets which is required. Our system is mainly targeted towards these kind of pet owners our system is as of now only made for dog owners and is mostly for diagnostics of dog skin disorders. Our system will ask questions to the dog owners about the dog’s skin and based on his skin dog owners will input the symptoms that there dog might be suffering from.
